No signals fired yet. The engine fires only when imbalance, acceleration and low exhaustion align.

How it fires — the EARLY BURST PHASE (upgraded): the engine reads each tick by the tick rule and fires at the birth of a move only when seven things agree: (1) TD% just broke the threshold, (2) acceleration just turned positive, (3) exhaustion is still low, (4) a first cluster of 2–6 ticks has formed, (5) trend alignment — the move agrees with the drift over the trend window (this is the big fix: it refuses to fire against the prevailing tape, which is what caused most reversal losses), (6) dual-window agreement — a shorter window confirms the imbalance isn't a one-off spike, and (7) expanding magnitude — ticks are growing, not dying. These extra filters fire less often but far more selectively.

Win / Loss: a WIN is a sustained 5-tick run in the fired direction — no meaningful reversal and net price progress the fired way. Flat ticks and sub-noise ticks (smaller than the noise-floor × average tick size) are tolerated, because a 0.00 or a −0.01 tick against a strong move is microstructure noise, not a real fall. Set the noise floor to 0 for the strict "5 strictly-consecutive" definition. The Edge vs Random tile compares the win rate to the 6.25% chance of four more like-ticks after a directional fire tick (0.5⁴).

Every tick, MACD(12,26,9), Choppiness(14), CCI(20) and Bollinger(20,2σ) are tracked in the background against the live price/time series. A row is written only when a run of 5 consecutive ticks forms. It shows the start time of the run, its direction, the price at which the run began and the 2nd–5th prices, then the indicator values observed at that run.

🎲 Digits prediction engine — —

Tracks the last digit (0–9) of every tick price in real time. Uses frequency deviation, gap analysis, a Markov transition matrix and a chi-square uniformity test to build a composite prediction score for each digit. Click any digit to place a manual Matches trade, or arm auto-execution to fire when the prediction engine's confidence exceeds your threshold.

📊 Prediction scores 0 ticks

Each cell shows the digit, its observed frequency %, composite prediction score, and gap (ticks since it last appeared). Higher bar = model favours this digit next. Click a digit to place a manual Matches trade.
Score formula: 40% frequency-deviation (digits below 10% get a boost) + 30% gap score (longer absent → higher) + 30% transition probability (Markov: P(digit | previous digit)). Scores are normalised so they sum to 100%. Blue border = top pick. Green = hot (above average). Red = cold (below average).
